Our Core Data Recovery Services

Hard Drive & SSD Recovery

Recovery from crashed, corrupted, mechanically failed, or logically damaged HDD and SSD drives across all major brands — WD, Seagate, Samsung, Toshiba, and more.

RAID & Server Recovery

Expert recovery for RAID 0, 1, 5, 6, 10, NAS, and SAN systems. We ensure minimal downtime for businesses with critical infrastructure failures.

Mobile & Tablet Recovery

Retrieval of lost contacts, messages, media, and app data from Android and iOS devices — including water-damaged or completely dead phones.

Flash & Memory Card Recovery

Recovery from USB drives, SD cards, CF cards, and microSD cards affected by accidental deletion, formatting, or severe corruption.

Ransomware Recovery

Specialized extraction from systems compromised by ransomware or malware attacks, with secure data sanitization to protect your information post-recovery.

Database & Forensic Recovery

Professional restoration for SQL and Oracle databases, plus legally compliant forensic recovery maintaining strict chain-of-custody protocols.
